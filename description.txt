Create huge arenas as fast as your computer can handle.

Adds:
 - Arena Builder
 Creates an arena spanning the entire world, starting at the top and going down to your cursor position. Platforms are placed every 100 blocks.
 - Hammered Arena Builder
 Same as the Arena Builder, but it also hammers all the platforms.
 - Walled Arena Builder
 Same as the Arena Builder, but it also adds background walls to the entire arena. Torches not included.
 - Full Arena Builder
 Combines the effects of the other Arena Builders.
